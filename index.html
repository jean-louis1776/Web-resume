About Me

Welcome to my official landing page!

My name is Ilya. I was born in Russia, Volgograd. I am 24 years old. I've always had a craving for technology. When I was a kid, I always wanted to know how various game consoles work.

When I was 13, my father bought me a guitar and I started making music. Later, my father and I founded a rock group that still exists today.

But interest in technology has not gone anywhere. So in 2017, I graduated from college and received my Computer Systems Specialist diploma. In college, I didn't like programming subjects. It seemed to me that it was boring and incomprehensible. But during the COVID-19 epidemic, the question of working from home arose. One evening a friend from Moscow called my father and asked how I was doing now. Then this friend suggested that I try to study as a Web developer. And I'm sure it wasn't just a random call.

And now I am student at GeekBrains IT-University at the Faculty of FullStack JavaScript Development. Now I have fully studied the Front-End direction and in October 2021 I started to study the Back-End direction. My favorite area is app development with ReactJS. In the future, I want to study cross-platform React applications in order to create not only Web versions of applications, but also Desktop and Mobile versions.

I would never have thought that I would enjoy programming. This is my favorite job!
